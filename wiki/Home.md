# Welcome to the AutoCYPAmber (ACYPA) Wiki

**AutoCYPAmber (ACYPA)** is an autonomous orchestration framework designed for high-fidelity molecular dynamics (MD) simulations of **Cytochrome P450 (CYP450)** systems.

Developed by **Ziyan Zhuang** and **Qianyu Zhao**, ACYPA bridges the gap between complex quantum mechanical (QM) calculations and large-scale classical simulations.

## 🚀 Core Philosophy
Traditional metalloprotein simulation requires months of manual parameterization. ACYPA reduces this to **minutes** of autonomous setup through:
- **High-Fidelity**: Native implementation of the **Shahrokh Protocol (2011)** for heme parameters.
- **Independence**: No Gaussian license required; 100% powered by **PySCF** and **Multiwfn**.
- **Autonomy**: Integrated **Agent-Skills** for direct orchestration by Large Language Models (LLMs).

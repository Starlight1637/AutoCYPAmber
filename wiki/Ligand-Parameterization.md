# Ligand Parameterization (QM/RESP)

High-fidelity ligand simulation requires **RESP (Restrained Electrostatic Potential)** charges based on the **HF/6-31G*** theory level.

## 1. The PySCF Pipeline
ACYPA replaces expensive, commercial Gaussian licenses with the open-source **PySCF** engine.
- **Workflow**: `Input (SDF/MOL2) -> PySCF (HF/6-31G*) -> .molden wavefunction`.
- **Convergence**: ACYPA automatically handles spin multiplicity (Singlet/Doublet) detection.

## 2. Automated Charge Injection
Once RESP charges are calculated via **Multiwfn**, ACYPA's `inject_resp_to_mol2` utility:
- Reads the GAFF2 atom-typed MOL2 generated by Antechamber.
- Replaces the BCC charges with QM-calculated RESP charges.
- Normalizes total system charge to an integer.

This ensures your ligand parameters are publication-quality.
